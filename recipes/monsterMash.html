Monster Mash Recipe

This meal is a staple in Stan Efferding's book 'The Verticle Diet'. It is a simple way to maximize calories and protein, but keep digestability.

Ingredients

  • Ground Bison
  • Bell Peppers
  • Spinach
  • Rice
  • Bone Broth
  • Spices and seasoning

Steps

  1. Cook the Rice
  2. Cook the Veggies
  3. Grill the ground beef
  4. Combine ingredients
  5. add Bone Broth for digestability
